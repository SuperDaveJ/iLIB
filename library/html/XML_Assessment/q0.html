Instructions

Welcome to the BESA I Final Exam!

There are 100 questions in this test.  All questions are multiple choice, with only one correct answer.

A portion of the questions are built around a scenario so you can answer the questions in context of an actual situation.  During this section of the test, you will be provided with Scenario Information screens, presented in between questions, to provide you with details of the scenario.

However, most of the scenario-based questions can be answered independently of the scenario so you should not worry about trying to memorize the details of the storyline.  The scenario is included simply to provide context.

You can navigate to a previous page in the test to review scenario details or to change your answer to a question. 

You have 2 hours to complete the exam.  If you have not completed the exam within 2 hours, the system will lock you out of the test and all progress will be lost.

If you select the Exit button before you complete the exam, progress will be lost and your score will not be tracked.

Good luck!
