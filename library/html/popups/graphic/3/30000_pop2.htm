How About a Little Support?

The course provides support tools that will help you apply the concepts of policy evaluation not only during your training but in your everyday job. Plenty of examples are provided throughout the course, as well as job aids and other tools that will be useful in conducting evaluation as it applies to policy. These resources generally are provided as web links or in portable document format (PDF).

You may view a list of all the resources cited in each lesson by clicking the “Resources” button at the bottom left of the screen. Clicking this button from inside the final lesson launches a consolidated list of all resources used in the course.
A list of terms used in the course can be accessed at any time by clicking the “Glossary” button at the bottom of the screen.

If you have problems navigating the course or have questions, please consult the Help page, accessible from the button at the bottom of the page.
